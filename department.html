Pediatrics

At Kigali Medical Center, we provide expert care for children from birth to adolescence. Our pediatric team offers services like vaccinations, check-ups, illness treatment, and growth monitoring in a safe, child-friendly environment. We’re here to support your child’s health every step of the way.
Healthy children,
happy families.

Dermatology at KMC

Kigali Medical Center offers expert care for skin, hair, and nail conditions. Our dermatologists treat acne, eczema, infections, and more, helping you maintain healthy skin with personalized, modern treatments.

Dentistry at KMC

Our dental team offers comprehensive oral care including check-ups, cleaning, fillings, extractions, and cosmetic treatments to keep your smile healthy and bright.

Allergy Services at KMC

We diagnose and treat allergies such as asthma, hay fever, food allergies, and skin reactions, helping you manage symptoms and improve your quality of life.

General Medicine at KMC

Our general medicine department provides thorough medical care for a wide range of health issues, focusing on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention for all ages.

Minor Surgery at KMC

We perform safe, outpatient minor surgical procedures with minimal downtime, including wound care, mole removals, and biopsies, all done with expert care.

Laboratory Services at KMC

Our modern laboratory offers fast and accurate diagnostic testing to support timely and precise medical decisions, including blood tests, urine analysis, and more.

Hospitalisation at KMC

We provide comfortable inpatient care with 24/7 monitoring, skilled nursing, and personalized treatment plans to support your recovery in a safe environment.
